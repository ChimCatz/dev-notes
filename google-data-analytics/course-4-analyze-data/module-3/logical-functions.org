* Course 4: Analyze Data — Logical Functions

** IF Function

*** Purpose
**** Perform conditional logic

*** Structure
**** IF(condition, value_if_true, value_if_false)

** Examples

*** Classify performance
**** High / Low revenue

*** Flag data
**** Valid / Invalid entries

** Nested Logic

*** Combine multiple IF statements
*** Handle complex conditions

** Benefit

*** Enables decision-making inside data
