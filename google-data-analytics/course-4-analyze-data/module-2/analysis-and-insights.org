* Course 4: Analyze Data — Analysis and Insights

** Analysis Flow

*** Organize
**** Group data (e.g., by year)

*** Aggregate
**** SUM, AVERAGE, COUNT

*** Filter
**** Focus on specific segments

*** Calculate
**** Percentages, comparisons

*** Interpret
**** Explain trends

** Example

*** 2015
**** Highest count
**** Lower average revenue

*** Insight
**** Higher % of low-performing entries

** Key Takeaway

*** Data alone is not enough
**** Analysts must interpret and explain results
