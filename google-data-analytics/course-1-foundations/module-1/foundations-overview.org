* Course 1: Foundations — Overview

** Data Analysis
*** Data analysis = turning data into insights
*** Goal: support decision-making using facts

** Data Analysis Process (6 Phases)

*** ASK
**** Define the problem
**** Identify stakeholders
**** Understand expectations

*** PREPARE
**** Collect data
**** Store data properly
**** Ensure fairness and accuracy

*** PROCESS
**** Clean data
**** Remove errors and duplicates
**** Transform into usable format

*** ANALYZE
**** Use tools (spreadsheets, SQL)
**** Identify patterns and trends

*** SHARE
**** Communicate insights clearly
**** Use visualizations

*** ACT
**** Apply insights to solve the problem

** Key Concepts

*** Data Ecosystem
**** Group of data that interact with each other

*** Cloud
**** Store and access data online

** Core Skills

*** Visualization
*** Strategy
*** Problem-solving
*** Correlation
*** Big-picture thinking
