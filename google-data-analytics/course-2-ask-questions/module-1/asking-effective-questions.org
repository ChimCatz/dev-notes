* Course 2: Ask Questions — Module 1

** Problem Solving

*** Key Idea
**** Define the problem clearly before analyzing data
**** Focus on root cause, not symptoms

*** SMART Questions
**** Specific → clear and focused
**** Measurable → quantifiable
**** Action-oriented → leads to action
**** Relevant → aligned to business goal
**** Time-bound → has deadline

** Types of Questions

*** Open-ended
**** Encourages detailed answers
**** Used for exploration

*** Closed-ended
**** Yes/No or fixed answers
**** Used for validation

** Stakeholders

*** Definition
**** People affected by the problem or solution

*** Goal
**** Understand needs and expectations

** Effective Communication

*** Ask clear and concise questions
*** Avoid assumptions
*** Confirm understanding

** Common Mistakes

*** Vague questions
*** Leading questions
*** Ignoring context
